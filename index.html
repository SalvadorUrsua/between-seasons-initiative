Why This Exists

Many people today live with anxiety, uncertainty, and financial pressure.

Between Seasons Initiative is a small effort to rediscover hope through faith, community, and stewardship of the land.

We believe that even small beginnings can grow into something meaningful for people and communities.

What We Are Exploring

Sustainable Land Practices

Learning simple and practical ways to care for the land and grow food sustainably.

Community Livelihood

Exploring small opportunities that can support families and local communities.

Faith and Reflection

Creating spaces for prayer, worship, encouragement, and spiritual renewal.
